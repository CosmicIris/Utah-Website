Family Activities

There are many activities in Utah that you can do with the kids, from interactive museums to zoos and aquariums. Three family activities to do with the kids include playing at the Discovery Gateway, learning about aquatic animals at the Loveland Living Planet Aquarium, and seeing a show at the Utah Childrenâ€™s Theatre.

The Discovery Gateway

The Discovery Gateway is a museum that provides interactive exhibits focusing on collaboration, problem solving, design, and creativity. Children can learn about financial literacy through The First Utah Bank Discovery Bank exhibit, experience the thrill of competition through the STEAM Studio and STEAM Lab, and discover the importance of bees through The Garden exhibit, among many more things.

Loveland Living Planet Aquarium



The Loveland Living Planet Aquarium is home to more than 4,000 animals from around the world and features five unique ecosystems to explore: Expedition Asia, Ocean Explorer, Discover Utah, Antarctic Adventure, and Journey to South America. Discover a variety of species, from Binturongs from South Asia to glowing sea jellyfish from Antarctica.
